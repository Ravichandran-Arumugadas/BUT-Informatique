On the program:

    Unveil the Incomparable Power of Crowds

  • Explore sociology, collective behavior, and the psychology of masses in a fascinating journey through history, politics, and science. Discover the impact of crowds on our society and delve into their complex interactions.

    • An Engaging Experience

  • Participate in innovative installations, captivating virtual experiences, and exciting demonstrations to better understand the profound social implications of mass phenomena.

    • A CONCERT!?

  • Join us for an exceptional interactive concert during the "Crowds" exhibition at the Cité des Sciences. Experience an immersive musical journey by singing along with our talented group of musicians and share your passion with other visitors for an unforgettable experience.

    • Discover the Fascinating World of Crowds

  • Don't miss this unique opportunity to discover a fascinating exhibition, rich in knowledge, interactivity, and surprises, dedicated to the study of crowds, one of the most captivating aspects of our modern society.

    • Animal Crowds in the Spotlight!

  • Dive into innovative interactive installations, captivating experiences, and exciting demonstrations to explore group dynamics, the social and scientific implications of crowds. A digital simulation of a school of fish, animal quizzes, and an interactive concert await you.

    • Explore the Mysterious World of Crowds

  • Explore urban mobility, the electric atmosphere of demonstrations, behind-the-scenes of major sports events, and significant political and cultural gatherings. Immerse yourself in the profound social implications stemming from these mass phenomena during this immersive exhibition.

    • Discover Crowds Like Never Before!

  • Dive into a unique experience at this interactive exhibition. Discover innovative installations, participate in captivating virtual experiences, and witness exciting demonstrations to better understand the characteristics and dynamics of crowds in various contexts.
